05 - Numere raționale (I)

Reguli de calcul​

Adunarea​

Regula 1
ab+cd=a⋅d+b⋅cb⋅d\frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a\cdot d + b \cdot c}{b \cdot d}ba​+dc​=b⋅da⋅d+b⋅c​

Exemplu:

25+37=2⋅7+3⋅55⋅7=14+1535=2935\frac{2}{5} + \frac{3}{7} = \frac{2 \cdot 7 + 3 \cdot 5}{5 \cdot 7} = \frac{14 + 15}{35} = \frac{29}{35}52​+73​=5⋅72⋅7+3⋅5​=3514+15​=3529​

Scăderea​

Regula 2
ab−cd=ad−bcbd\frac{a}{b} - \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad - bc}{bd}ba​−dc​=bdad−bc​

Exemplu:

57−23=5⋅3−2⋅77⋅3=15−1421=121\frac{5}{7} - \frac{2}{3} = \frac{5 \cdot 3 - 2 \cdot 7}{7 \cdot 3} = \frac{15 - 14}{21} = \frac{1}{21}75​−32​=7⋅35⋅3−2⋅7​=2115−14​=211​

Înmulțirea​

Regula 3
ab⋅cd=a⋅cb⋅d\frac{a}{b} \cdot \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a \cdot c}{b \cdot d}ba​⋅dc​=b⋅da⋅c​

Exemplu:

53⋅27=1021\frac{5}{3} \cdot \frac{2}{7} = \frac{10}{21}35​⋅72​=2110​

Împărțirea​

Regula 4
ab:cd=ab⋅dc=a⋅db⋅c\frac{a}{b} : \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a}{b} \cdot \frac{d}{c} = \frac{a \cdot d}{b \cdot c}ba​:dc​=ba​⋅cd​=b⋅ca⋅d​

Exemplu:

52:27=52⋅72=354\frac{5}{2} : \frac{2}{7} = \frac{5}{2} \cdot \frac{7}{2} = \frac{35}{4}25​:72​=25​⋅27​=435​

Ridicarea la putere​

Regula 5
(ab)n=anbn\left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^n = \frac{a^n}{b^n}(ba​)n=bnan​

Exemplu:

(25)3=2353=8125\left( \frac{2}{5} \right)^3 = \frac{2^3}{5^3} = \frac{8}{125}(52​)3=5323​=1258​

Numere negative​

Regula 6
−ab=−ab=a−b- \frac{a}{b} = \frac{-a}{b} = \frac{a}{-b}−ba​=b−a​=−ba​

Exemplu:

−37=−37=3−7- \frac{3}{7} = \frac{-3}{7} = \frac{3}{-7}−73​=7−3​=−73​

Înmulțirea ridicărilor la putere​

Regula 7
(ab)n⋅(ab)m=(ab)n+m\left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^n \cdot \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^m = \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^{n + m}(ba​)n⋅(ba​)m=(ba​)n+m

Exemplu:

(25)2⋅(25)3=(25)5\left( \frac{2}{5} \right)^2 \cdot \left( \frac{2}{5} \right)^3 = \left( \frac{2}{5} \right)^5(52​)2⋅(52​)3=(52​)5

Împărțirea ridicărilor la putere​

Regula 8
(ab)n:(ab)m=(ab)n−m\left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^n : \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^m = \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^{n - m}(ba​)n:(ba​)m=(ba​)n−m

Exemplu:

(35)3:(35)2=(35)1=35\left( \frac{3}{5} \right)^3 : \left( \frac{3}{5} \right)^2 = \left( \frac{3}{5} \right)^1 = \frac{3}{5}(53​)3:(53​)2=(53​)1=53​

Reguli speciale​

De reținut
R1:(ab)0=1R_1: \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^0 = 1R1​:(ba​)0=1R2:(ab)1=abR_2: \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^1 = \frac{a}{b}R2​:(ba​)1=ba​
