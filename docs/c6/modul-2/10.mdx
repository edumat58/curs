# C15 - Reguli de calcul cu puteri

---

## 1.1. Regula 1

$$a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}$$

### Exemple

- $2^3 \cdot 2^4 = 2^{3+4} = 2^7 = 128$
- $3^5 \cdot 3^2 = 3^{5+2} = 3^7 = 2187$

---

## 1.2. Regula 2

$$\frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n}$$

### Exemple

- $\frac{2^5}{2^2} = 2^{5-2} = 2^3 = 8$
- $\frac{3^7}{3^3} = 3^{7-3} = 3^4 = 81$

---
