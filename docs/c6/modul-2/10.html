C15 - Reguli de calcul cu puteri

1.1. Regula 1​

am⋅an=am+na^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}am⋅an=am+n

Exemple​

  • 23⋅24=23+4=27=1282^3 \cdot 2^4 = 2^{3+4} = 2^7 = 12823⋅24=23+4=27=128
  • 35⋅32=35+2=37=21873^5 \cdot 3^2 = 3^{5+2} = 3^7 = 218735⋅32=35+2=37=2187

1.2. Regula 2​

aman=am−n\frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n}anam​=am−n

Exemple​

  • 2522=25−2=23=8\frac{2^5}{2^2} = 2^{5-2} = 2^3 = 82225​=25−2=23=8
  • 3733=37−3=34=81\frac{3^7}{3^3} = 3^{7-3} = 3^4 = 813337​=37−3=34=81

1.3. Regula 3​

(am)n=am⋅n(a^m)^n = a^{m \cdot n}(am)n=am⋅n

Exemple​

  • (23)4=23⋅4=212=4096(2^3)^4 = 2^{3 \cdot 4} = 2^{12} = 4096(23)4=23⋅4=212=4096
