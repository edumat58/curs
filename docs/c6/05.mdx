# 05 - Numere raționale (I)
---
## Reguli de calcul
### Adunarea
:::danger Regula 1
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a\cdot d + b \cdot c}{b \cdot d}`}</Math>
:::
**Exemplu:**

<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\frac{2}{5} + \frac{3}{7} = \frac{2 \cdot 7 + 3 \cdot 5}{5 \cdot 7} = \frac{14 + 15}{35} = \frac{29}{35}`}</Math>

---

### Scăderea
:::danger Regula 2
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\frac{a}{b} - \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad - bc}{bd}`}</Math>
:::
**Exemplu:**

<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\frac{5}{7} - \frac{2}{3} = \frac{5 \cdot 3 - 2 \cdot 7}{7 \cdot 3} = \frac{15 - 14}{21} = \frac{1}{21}`}</Math>

---

### Înmulțirea
:::danger Regula 3
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\frac{a}{b} \cdot \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a \cdot c}{b \cdot d}`}</Math>
:::
**Exemplu:**

<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\frac{5}{3} \cdot \frac{2}{7} = \frac{10}{21}`}</Math>

---

### Împărțirea
:::danger Regula 4
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\frac{a}{b} : \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a}{b} \cdot \frac{d}{c} = \frac{a \cdot d}{b \cdot c}`}</Math>
:::
**Exemplu:**

<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\frac{5}{2} : \frac{2}{7} = \frac{5}{2} \cdot \frac{7}{2} = \frac{35}{4}`}</Math>

---

### Ridicarea la putere
:::danger Regula 5
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^n = \frac{a^n}{b^n}`}</Math>
:::
**Exemplu:**

<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\left( \frac{2}{5} \right)^3 = \frac{2^3}{5^3} = \frac{8}{125}`}</Math>

---

### Numere negative
:::danger Regula 6
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`- \frac{a}{b} = \frac{-a}{b} = \frac{a}{-b}`}</Math>
:::
**Exemplu:**

<Math display={true}>{String.raw`- \frac{3}{7} = \frac{-3}{7} = \frac{3}{-7}`}</Math>

---

### Înmulțirea ridicărilor la putere
:::danger Regula 7
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^n \cdot \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^m = \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^{n + m}`}</Math>
:::
**Exemplu:**

<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\left( \frac{2}{5} \right)^2 \cdot \left( \frac{2}{5} \right)^3 = \left( \frac{2}{5} \right)^5`}</Math>

---

### Împărțirea ridicărilor la putere
:::danger Regula 8
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^n : \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^m = \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^{n - m}`}</Math>
:::
**Exemplu:**

<Math display={true}>{String.raw`\left( \frac{3}{5} \right)^3 : \left( \frac{3}{5} \right)^2 = \left( \frac{3}{5} \right)^1 = \frac{3}{5}`}</Math>

---

### Reguli speciale
:::danger De reținut
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`R_1: \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^0 = 1`}</Math>  
<Math display={true}>{String.raw`R_2: \left( \frac{a}{b} \right)^1 = \frac{a}{b}`}</Math>
:::
