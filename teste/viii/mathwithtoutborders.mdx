### Exercise 1 - Who's next? (7 points)

Mr and Mrs Smith live together with their four children in a large house with two bathrooms.
They like to have breakfast together as a family, which makes the two bathrooms crowded and difficult to use.
Mrs Smith uses the bathroom for 21 minutes, and Mr Smith for 15 minutes.
Their children Emily, Oliver, Harry, and Lily use the bathroom for 14, 8, 13, and 7 minutes respectively.

The Smith family has breakfast for 20 minutes.
Everyone leaves the house at 8 a.m.

Knowing that each family member likes to sleep as long as possible, at what time must the first person go to the bathroom?
How can the family best organize the use of the bathrooms? Give one possible schedule.

### Exercise 3 - The mentalist (7 points)

**Evelyn is a mentalist; she claims she can read other people’s thoughts.**
She hands a calculator to Thomas and says:
– Choose a three-digit whole number, without telling me.
– Multiply it by 21.
– Multiply the result, as you like, by 3 or 4 or 5 or 6.
– Divide this result by 4, then by the number you chose at the start.
– Add the number you first chose to this result.
– Read out to me the number shown on the calculator.

**Evelyn then looks Thomas in the eyes and tells him the number he had chosen.
Explain how Evelyn did it.**
